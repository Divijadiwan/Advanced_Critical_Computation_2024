Medicinal

Many people use Sleepytime tea do help them rest at night. Sleepytime includes ingredients like chamomile to calm the nervous system and result in better sleep. For centuries people have been using tea as a relaxing agent or even for medicinal purposes. White tea, for example, has been shown to be effective in fighting certain form of cancer due to its antioxidants. Peppermint tea has menthol which helps with upset stomachs, constipation, and headaches. Green tea helps lower bad cholesterol, improving heart health. It also has anti-inflammatory properties. Tea in general is also very helpful for treating sore throats.
